The RISCS Ratings SystemTM

The RISC Rating SystemTM is the first and only platform that uses non-public manufacturer-reported data to provide robust supply chain analytics at the NDC level.

The RISC Ratings Portal enables pharmaceutical manufacturers and healthcare professionals from group purchasing organizations and health systems to submit, edit, or view supply chain data for critical pharmaceuticals.

The system is developed to help GPOs or Hospital or Drug Manufacturers to know more about the availability and rating of the drug. The rating is awarded based on some complex computation which takes into consideration several factors like number of raw material supplier, geographic distribution, stock, shelf life and so much more.

Field Pros Direct

Field Pros Direct is an automated assignment platform that matches experienced adjusters and service providers with claims based on customized preferences. A tech-enabled claims solution to assist every aspect of the claims process - from start to finish.

Field adjusters, desk adjusters and other claims service providers are matched with assignments based on expertise. The smart-matching method helps ensure accurate indemnity, while eliminating litigation expenses.

Field Pros Direct brings all service providers under one umbrella, and provides an on demand claim adjuster, and thus facilitating everything end to end from Servicer Provider, Adjuster and the end customer perspective. The system is seamless and automated. Automated claim assignment, appointment scheduling, automated communication through emails and sms, automated report generation, claim settlement, invoice generation and much more.
